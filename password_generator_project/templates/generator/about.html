This app was created as a learning project. Here you can generate different passwords:

  • - different length;
  • - you can create one password or 5 or 10;
  • - only numbers;
  • - capital letter only;
  • - only lowercase letters;
  • - only special characters;
  • - or any combination of the above.

The main purpose of this application is to help you choose a password for your purpose.

  1. click on the password generator link;
  2. choose the length of the password;
  3. choose an account password;
  4. select the desired characters for your password;
  5. click the generate password button.
